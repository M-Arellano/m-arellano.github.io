GTKFA

This site was developed for Golden Tiger Kung Fu Academy using Wordpress and Elementor. Wordpress was chosen to facilitate an easier update process for the GTKFA staff.

The course offerings quickly grew to meet the varying needs of the students after the pandemic hit. The limitations of the theme previously chosen did not meet their needs for the additional pages that were needed to support some of the new course offerings. To meet the needs of their growing course offerings some additional hand coded pages were added.

Rather than redesigning the site from scratch the new hand coded pages were seamlessly integrated into the current theme. This enabled GTKFA to get the pages they needed up and running as quickly as possible at the lowest possible cost.

Audrey Shi Realty

This is a single page scrolling site, hand coded with a parallax effect. Utilizing media queries, the design is fully responsive and optimized for mobile view, tablets in landscape or portrait mode, laptops, and large screen displays of 1900px+.
